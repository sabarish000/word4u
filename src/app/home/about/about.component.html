About word4u

Player1 thinks of a 4 letters word (For example WORD) and enter it in Player1 screen
NOTE: Word should not have repeative letters

Player2 has to guess the word.

If the guess matches game ends.

Otherwise it shows the number of matching letters with actual word.
For Example Player2 guessed BIRD, it has two matching letters with WORD.

Then Players can exchange thier roles and who guesses with minimum chances is the WINNER
